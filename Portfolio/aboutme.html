ABOUT ME

-Sarah Kaminski-

Sarah is an Ohio native and has a great passion for learning, knowlegde, and the arts. If she's not reading a new book she is either writing, painting, or dancing to a favorite tune. She is an outgoing individual who chases and embraces change and personal growth in all avenues.

She has mastered the art of baking cheesecake and making a flavorful chili both from scratch. Her creativity and passion for the arts transitions well into the world of Computer Programming. Front-end developing comes naturally to her, while the back-end developing provides her the challanges she is constantly searching for.

After nearly 4 years in sales, she has finally taken the leap into Software Engineering and a career path she loves. She is currently an apprentice at We Can Code IT and has been focusing on C#, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript but looks forward to learning new languages as well.


Skills

Emphasis on C# and Object Oriented Programming, .NET/ASP.net, HTML5, CSS, Bootstrap, jQuery, SQL, MVC, JavaScript, responsive design/Mobile, Transactional Databases/MSSQL, Source Control, Unity & Git.
